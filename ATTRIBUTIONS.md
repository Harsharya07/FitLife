# Image Attributions

Images in `frontend/public/images/` were downloaded from [Pexels](https://www.pexels.com) and [Unsplash](https://unsplash.com) under their free licenses.

- **Pexels License:** Free for personal and commercial use, no attribution required.
- **Unsplash License:** Free for personal and commercial use.

To refresh assets after adding new content entries, run:

```bash
python3 scripts/download_images.py
```

Then update paths in `backend/app/data/` to match filenames under `frontend/public/images/`.
